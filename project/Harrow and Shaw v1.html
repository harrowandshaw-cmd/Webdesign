II · ANATOMY

A shoe, dissected.

Two hundred and seven pieces. Thirty-one operations. What follows is a slow walk around the bench — the names our cordwainers give each part of a pair.

PART № 01

The Vamp

Cut in one piece from the belly of the hide, the vamp carries the shoe's silhouette. We choose it from the softest third of the leather and skive it by hand to a feather-thin edge.

III · BESPOKE

A pair, made to the shape of you.

Commissioning a bespoke pair is a slow conversation. You will sit with Mr. Shaw for the first fitting, return six weeks later for the trial, and wait roughly eighteen months for your finished pair. Your last — a wooden sculpture of your foot — remains with us for life.
